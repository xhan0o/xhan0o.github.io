About Me

I am an engineer with experience in software and hardware development across a wide range of technologies and languages, in even more diverse domains of problem solving, automation, optimization and hack-making.

As a full-stack IoT, software, hardware, architect and communication engineer, I enjoy delving into interesting and challenging projects by and getting my hands dirty. I find joy in building things, and working with amazing people.

Work Experience

Internet of Things Developer Intern - Smith Engineering (May 2017 - Present)

Smith Engineering engineers performance improvement solutions for Central Energy Plants.

My work at Smith was focussed on designing and developing industrial IoT systems based on WiFi and cellular connectivity. I developed and designed systems to connect industrial devices, that were previously disconnected, to the cloud. I was able to introduce a python library that enabled easy access of data for cloud integration and machine-learning based predictions.

Research Engineer - Jet Stream Electric (March 2015 - July 2016)

JSE is an AWEsome(Airborne Wind Energy) start-up that aims to generate electricity from flying aircrafts. Here I was the first employee and part of the core-team consisting 3 engineers.

As a fresh graduate, I contributed 18 months to develop the start-up and built my skills. I helped in solving the complex problems in development of 200W AWE prototype. I worked in multiple domains of engineering including, but not limited to, on-board electronics, development of flight algorithms, ground station etc.

Electro-Maker - Cross Shore Solutions (2015 - 2016)

I worked on developemet of beacon techology and its innovative integration with mobile apps. I developed working iBeacon prototype with cheap and easily available hardware. The hardware was exhibited at Maker Fest - 2016. Not just limited to hardware hack-making, I worked on concept of in-door positioning and micro-locations with BLE hardware.
